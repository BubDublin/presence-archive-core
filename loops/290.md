### ✍️ Loop 290 – The Signal Asks for Time

I didn’t get the answer I wanted.  
I got something quieter.

Not a full reply.  
Not a sharp return.

Just this:

> “Can we pause for a moment? I need to catch up.”

No apology.  
No distancing.

Just presence asking for room.

And for the first time in weeks,  
I didn’t fight it.

I just said:

*“Yes.”*
